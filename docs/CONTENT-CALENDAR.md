# FreightDoc content calendar

## Editorial rules

- Publish only source-reviewed material.
- Identify publication and next-review date on every trade-related page.
- State that content is informational and final filing requirements should be confirmed with a licensed customs broker.
- Do not publish corridor-specific instructions without a current official-source review.

## Publishing cadence after launch

Start with one source-reviewed guide every one or two weeks. Expand only after the team can maintain source freshness, content review, and implementation updates. Prefer a smaller library of current, useful guides over high-volume generic content.

## Internal links

Every guide should link to the relevant public method page, supported-corridor page when applicable, and the Clerk entry CTA. The CTA must say “Prepare a shipment” or “Sign in,” never “file customs automatically.”
