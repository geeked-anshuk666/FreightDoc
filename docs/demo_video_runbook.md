# Demo video runbook

Record one clean 90–120 second take with voiceover. Use a fresh browser profile, one readable zoom level, no notifications, pre-warmed services, and only the synthetic shipment from the README. Do not hide failures: if a provider is unavailable, show the deterministic/manual path.

| Time | Screen state and action | Spoken words |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:10 | Landing/workspace with the safety boundary visible. | “FreightDoc turns a shipment brief into a reviewable export-documentation dossier before a human reviewer makes the final call.” |
| 0:10–0:27 | Open the seeded Aurora earbuds shipment; keep route and value visible. | “I start with synthetic shipment facts: origin, destination, product, quantity, and commercial details. These remain reviewable records, not an AI answer treated as final.” |
| 0:27–0:46 | Requirements or classification/evidence state. | “FreightDoc applies deterministic route rules and shows evidence and fallback status behind its classification and document requirements.” |
| 0:46–1:06 | Draft/revision and quality finding; show reviewer action. | “It drafts and validates the dossier, making gaps visible. A reviewer can accept, correct, or waive a finding with a recorded decision.” |
| 1:06–1:20 | PDF dossier/export state. | “When it is internally review-ready, the team can export a dossier with its supporting context. That is preparation, not customs filing, clearance, or legal advice.” |
| 1:20–1:33 | Return to quality/closing product state. | “The core workflow works without an AI key. When optional suggestions are enabled, a person still remains accountable.” |
| 1:33–1:48 | Clearly labelled development-tool disclosure card; do not show private session content. | “Codex: [verified work]. GPT-5.6: [verified work]. [Only state a model variant or effort level if a local record displays it.] Runtime AI is separately configured and optional.” |
| 1:48–2:00 | Clean closing screen. | “FreightDoc makes export-document preparation more reviewable, traceable, and practical for teams moving goods across borders.” |

## Recording checklist

- Rehearse once without recording; confirm each named screen exists at the submitted revision.
- Keep cursor movements deliberate and wait for visible state changes.
- Upload to YouTube as public or unlisted; play it back once.
- The disclosure slot is mandatory for the Build Week video but must be populated from verified local evidence. Capture `/feedback` separately for Devpost.
