# FreightDoc — Build Week submission notes

FreightDoc prepares a reviewable export-documentation dossier from a shipment brief. It is deterministic-first preparation software, not a broker, filing, clearance, sanctions-screening, or legal service.

## What to demonstrate

Show synthetic shipment facts, deterministic requirements/evidence, a reviewable finding or revision, and a PDF dossier. Explain that optional runtime suggestions are separately configured and human reviewed.

Source: Build Week email/checklist supplied to the team, reviewed 2026-07-21.
